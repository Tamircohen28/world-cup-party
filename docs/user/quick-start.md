# Quick Start

Get from zero to your first watch party and prediction in about 5 minutes.

## 1. Open the app

Navigate to the deployed app URL (or run it locally — see the [README](../../README.md)).

## 2. Create an account

Click **Register** and fill in your display name, email, and password. Your display name is what other users see on the leaderboard and in party attendee lists.

## 3. Browse upcoming matches

The **Home** screen shows:
- Days until the tournament opens (June 11, 2026)
- Your next upcoming attended match
- Matches this week that you haven't RSVP'd to

Tap **Games** in the bottom nav to see the full schedule, filterable by group, stage, or team.

## 4. RSVP to a match

Open any match and tap **Attending**, **Maybe**, or **Not Attending**. Your RSVPs are saved and used to show you personalised reminders.

## 5. Join or create a watch party

From any match detail page:
- If a watch party already exists, tap **Join Party** to see location, attendees, and the items list
- If no party exists yet, tap **Host a Party** to set a location and notes

Claim something from the bring-items list so the host knows what's covered.

## 6. Submit a prediction

From the match detail page, tap **Predict**. Enter the final score you expect, then optionally fill in the bonus bets for extra points. Predictions lock at kickoff — submit early.

## 7. Check the leaderboard

Tap **Leaderboard** to see where you stand after results are entered. Tap your own row to see a full breakdown of your prediction history and earned badges.
